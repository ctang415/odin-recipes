Cheeseburger

Description

Cheeseburgers are an American staple. A simple yet delicious meal made up of a beef patty with cheese, tomatoes, pickles, and lettuce held together by two buns.

Ingredients

  • Beef patty
  • American cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Pickles
  • Buns

Steps

  1. Heat up the beef patty on a grill to your desired rarity.
  2. Add cheese on top of patty to melt it.
  3. Remove and place the patty on bun and begin adding the rest of the tomatoes, pickles, and lettuce on top.
  4. Put the other bun on top and enjoy your burger!
