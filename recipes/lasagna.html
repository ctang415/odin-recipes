Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is not spaghetti, but I only know how to make spaghetti, so that's what we're making. It's a simple Italian dish made up of noodles, tomato sauce, and meatballs.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Pasta sauce
  • Cheese
  • Meatballs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 and toss meatballs inside for 30 minutes.
  2. Boil water, use salt, and put noodles inside for 10 minutes.
  3. Heat up pasta sauce until boiling.
  4. Serve and enjoy with cheese if you want!
