Gyudon

Description

Simple Japanese dish that is made of rice, beef, and onions topped with soy sauce.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Beef slices
  • Onion
  • Soy Sauce
  • Mirin
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Mix 1 teaspoon of sugar, 2 tablespoons of soy sauce, and 2 table spoons of mirin in a bowl.
  2. Soak the raw slices of beef into the marinade.
  3. Cut onion into half slices and mix into marinade.
  4. Wash rice.
  5. Use rice cooker with a separator to cook both the rice and marinated beef at the same time.
